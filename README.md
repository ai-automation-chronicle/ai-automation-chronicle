# AI Automation Chronicle

A weekly blog series covering AI and automation research - deep-dives into papers and practical workflows.

## Content

**Research & Ideas** - Weekly breakdowns of AI/ML research papers with real benchmarks, formulas, and findings.

**AI & Automation in Practice** - Practical articles on tools, workflows, and automation systems.
